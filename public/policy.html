FCO Alerts: Privacy Policy

The Android application FCO Alerts requires access to certain permissions on your device in order to function. Although it asks for the "accounts" permission, please be assured that no personal details are retrieved from the device. It is purely to retrieve an account identifier for use with the Google Cloud Message system, which is used for the alert notifications you receive on your phone.

No personal or sensitive data is retrieved or stored by this application. We only store any country preferences you have set, and an identifier returned to us from the GCM system that allows us to send alerts to your device.
